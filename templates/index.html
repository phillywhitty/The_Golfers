The K Club

County Kildare

The K Club offers a unique golfing experience with two parkland courses designed by Arnold Palmer, the only 36-hole Palmer-designed destination in Europe. Both courses have received significant upgrades. Proudly hosting the 2006 Ryder Cup, our golf team looks forward to the future with a trailblazing attitude on our 550-acre resort.

Ballybunnion

County Kerry

Located on the north-west coast of County Kerry, on a beautiful stretch of sand dunes overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, you’ll find two wonderfully challenging and unique world-class links golf courses at Ballybunion – the Old Course and the Trent Jones Cashen Course. Whatever your choice, every hole, every hazard and every shot is defined by nature’s infinite presence.

Trump International

County Clare

Trump International Golf Links Doonbeg features a stunning Dr. Martin Hawtree-designed course along a 1.5-mile crescent-shaped beach. Enjoy Atlantic views on 16 of 18 holes in this par-72 layout spanning 7,026 yards. The round typically takes four and a half hours. The course, following Norman's 'least disturbance' philosophy, weaves naturally through sand dunes, offering a unique mix of five par 3s and five par 5s
